Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

BeautyTask ("we," "our," or "us") operates the BeautyTask mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and password
  • Profile Information: Name, profile picture, and contact details
  • Job/Product Information: Job postings, product listings, and related content

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates when you enable location services
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, features used, and time spent in the app
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Google Play In-App Purchase

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide and maintain our Service
  • Process job postings and product listings
  • Handle payments for premium features
  • Send notifications about relevant opportunities
  • Improve our Service and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

We use Supabase for secure data storage and Firebase for push notifications. Your data is protected by:

  • Encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and monitoring

5. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services
  • Firebase: Push notifications
  • Google Play Services: In-app purchases and location services
  • Google Maps: Location and mapping features

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal requirements
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • With service providers who assist in app operations

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Export your data

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our Service and comply with legal obligations. When you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your BeautyTask account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, profile information
  • Job Postings: All jobs you have posted
  • Product Listings: All products you have listed for sale
  • Messages: All chat messages and conversations
  • Photos: All images uploaded to your posts
  • Location Data: Saved locations and GPS coordinates
  • App Preferences: Settings and customizations
  • Payment History: Transaction records (for accounting purposes, some anonymized data may be retained)

What Data May Be Retained

Some data may be retained for legal or business purposes:

  • Anonymized Analytics: Usage statistics without personal identifiers
  • Legal Compliance: Data required by law or for dispute resolution
  • Security Logs: Access logs for security purposes (retained for 90 days)
  • Backup Data: Encrypted backups may exist for up to 30 days

Data Retention Period

After account deletion:

  • Immediate Deletion: Most personal data is deleted within 24 hours
  • Backup Cleanup: All backups are permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Analytics: Anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for service improvement

Important Notes

  • Account deletion is permanent and irreversible
  • You will lose access to all your posted jobs and products
  • Any active transactions or pending payments will be cancelled
  • You can create a new account with the same email address after deletion
  • We may contact you once to confirm the deletion request

Alternative: Deactivate Account

If you're unsure about permanent deletion, you can:

  • Deactivate your account: Temporarily disable your account without deleting data
  • Contact support: Discuss your concerns with our support team
  • Modify privacy settings: Adjust what information is visible to others
